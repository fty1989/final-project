About Us

Mission

To provide Unique goods and services at afforable prices
for the people of Navrongo and its surrounding towns

Vision

To contribute sustainably to the unemployment deficit in the Sub Region
by providing both direct and indirect employment opportunities to the youth
of Navrongo, its surrounded communities and the Upper East Region at larged.
To also contribute to infranstructure development in the sub region

History

Willy A. Ventures was established in the year 2011 at Navrongo in the UER by Mr. Wilfred Anecham

He started with the production of sacket mineral water with the brand name
Unique Natural Mineral Water. And had a total number of 5 employees.
Today, Unique has become a family name for the products and services Willy A. Ventures provide
Which includes:

  • Unique Hostel
  • Unique Rental Services
  • Unique Constructions & building materials
  • Unique Fish Production

Today, Willy A. Ventures has over 30 employees and one branch in the Ashanti Region (Kwamo)

Unique mineral water

Unique Natural Mineral Water is produce in a clean and hygienic environment
and satisfied by FDA. It one of the best mineral water in Upper East Region.
